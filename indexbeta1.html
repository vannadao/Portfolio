Vanna Dao is from California, United States. At a young age, she loves to draw cartoons and Japanese animations characters and watch movies and shows. This small hobby soon became her motivation to become an illustrator, animator, and video editor. Vanna graduated from San Jose State University in Fall 2020. She is currently working at the Museum of Art and Digital Entertainment. She also has a small freelance business where she accepts commissions for emotes, both still and animated.
